SIRS Compare · Open school-location sources

Compare open school-location datasets over high-resolution satellite imagery for five West African countries. Toggle sources on and off and zoom in to a school to eyeball whether each point sits on a real school building - a quick way to gauge the completeness and accuracy of any one source before relying on it. Giga (UNICEF) school data is intentionally excluded from this public app and is compared in a separate private variant.

What the points are - all are single points (one per school record), not building footprints:
  • OpenStreetMap: amenity=school - a hand-placed node, or the centroid of a school-grounds/building polygon.
  • Overture Maps: places POIs (from commercial datasets) tagged school/education - so the "school" definition is looser.
Points are clipped to each country's admin-0 boundary. Counts are not de-duplicated across sources.
